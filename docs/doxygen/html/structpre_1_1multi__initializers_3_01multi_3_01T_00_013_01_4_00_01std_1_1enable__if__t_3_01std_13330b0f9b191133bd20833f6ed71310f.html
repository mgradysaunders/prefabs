Parameters
[in]uSample in \( [0, 1)^2 \).
Expression

\[ \omega_{\text{hemisphere}}(\mathbf{u}) = \begin{bmatrix} \sin(\theta) \cos(\phi) \\ \sin(\theta) \sin(\phi) \\ \cos(\theta) \end{bmatrix} \]

where
  • \( \cos(\theta) \gets u_{[0]} \)
  • \( \sin(\theta) \gets \sqrt{1 - \cos^2(\theta)} \)
  • \( \phi \gets 2 \pi u_{[1]} \)
