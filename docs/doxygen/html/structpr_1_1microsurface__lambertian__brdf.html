Single-scattering BRDF.

\[ f_s(\omega_o, \omega_i) = \frac{\langle{\omega_m, \omega_i}\rangle}{\pi} \frac{1 + \Lambda(\omega_o)} {1 + \Lambda(\omega_o) + \Lambda(\omega_i)} L_0 \]

where \( \omega_m \sim D_{\omega_o} \)

Parameters
[in]uSample in \( [0, 1)^2 \).
[in]woOutgoing direction.
[in]wiIncident direction.
