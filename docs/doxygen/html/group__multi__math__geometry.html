\( L^2 \) length, fast (and somewhat unsafe) variant.

\[ \sqrt{\sum_k |x_{[k]}|^2} \]

Fast means the implementation

  • assumes no overflow/underflow,
  • does not calculate the absolute values of complex numbers separately.
