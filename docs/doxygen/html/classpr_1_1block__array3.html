Detailed Description

template<typename T, typename Talloc = std::allocator<T>>
class pr::block_array3< T, Talloc >

Block array (3-dimensional).

Block array with block size \( B = 2^k \) for an integer \( k > 0 \), which is chosen to promote cache performance. Specifically, \( k \) is chosen such that each \( B^2 \) block fits on an L1 cache line which, by default, the implementation assumes to be 64 bytes. To change this assumption, define L1_LINE to the appropriate size before including the data structure.

Note
The implementation blocks only the 0th and 1st dimensions. As a result, the addresses of adjacent locations in the 2nd dimension are usually far apart.

3-dimensional index to 1-dimensional index.

3-dimensional index \( (l_0, l_1, l_2) \) to 1-dimensional index \( j \). In particular,

\[ j = B^2 (q_0 + (n_0 / B) q_1) + (r_0 + B r_1) + n_0 n_1 l_2 \]

where the \( q_k, r_k \) denote the quotients and remainders of the \( l_k / B \) respectively.

1-dimensional index to 3-dimensional index.

1-dimensional index \( j \) to 3-dimensional index \( (l_0, l_1, l_2) \). In particular,

\[ \begin{aligned} l_0 &= B q_0 + r_0 \\ l_1 &= B q_1 + r_1 \\ l_2 &= s \end{aligned} \]

where

  • \( q_0 \) and \( q_1 \) denote the remainder and quotient of \( q / (n_0 / B) \) respectively, and
  • \( r_0 \) and \( r_1 \) denote the remainder and quotient of \( r / B \) respectively,

where, in turn,

  • \( q \) and \( r \) denote the quotient and remainder of \( t / B^2 \) respectively, and
  • \( s \) and \( t \) denote the quotient and remainder of \( j / (n_0 n_1) \) respectively.
