template<typename T >
std::enable_if_t< std::is_floating_point<T>::value, multi<T, 2> > pr::uniform_disk ( multi< T, 2 >  u)
inline

Uniform disk.

\[ \mathbf{P}_{\text{disk}}(\mathbf{u}) = \begin{bmatrix} r \cos{\theta} \\ r \sin{\theta} \end{bmatrix} \]

where

  • \( u_{[0]}' \gets 2 u_{[0]} - 1 \)
  • If \( |u_{[0]}'| > |u_{[1]}| \):

    \[ (r, \theta) = \left(u_{[0]}', \frac{\pi}{4}\frac{u_{[1]}}{u_{[0]}'}\right) \]

  • If \( |u_{[0]}'| \le |u_{[1]}| \):

    \[ (r, \theta) = \left(u_{[1]}, \frac{\pi}{2} - \frac{\pi}{4}\frac{u_{[0]}'}{u_{[1]}}\right) \]

Parameters
[in]uSample in \( [0, 1)^2 \).
