Look-at.

  • \( \mathbf{p}_c \gets \mathbf{p}_0 \)
  • \( \hat{\mathbf{z}} \gets \normalize(\mathbf{p}_0 - \mathbf{p}_1) \)
  • \( \hat{\mathbf{x}} \gets \normalize( -\mathbf{y}_0\times \hat{\mathbf{z}}) \)
  • \( \hat{\mathbf{y}} \gets \hat{\mathbf{z}} \times \hat{\mathbf{x}} \)
Parameters
[in]p0Point from.
[in]p1Point to.
[in]y0Up vector.
[in]sxScreen size.
[in]syScreen size.
[in]fY-FOV in radians.
[in]qminSub-region bounding point.
[in]qmaxSub-region boundint point.
Note
For convenience, the implementation crosses with \( -\mathbf{y}_0 \), such that the camera coordinate system is upside-down (since pixel coordinates increase downward).

Importance weight.

\[ Z_e(\mathbf{p}, \omega) = \delta(\mathbf{p} - \mathbf{p}_c) \frac{1}{A\cos^3\theta} \]

Parameters

Note

Importance weight solid angle sampling routine.

With respect to a reference point \( \mathbf{p}_{\text{ref}} \), sample a direction \( \omega_i \) within the solid-angle the camera subtends, and calculate the corresponding importance \( Z_i \).

\[ Z_i(\omega_i; \mathbf{p}_{\text{ref}}) = \frac{1}{d^2} Z_e(\mathbf{p}_{\text{hit}}, -\omega_i) \]

Parameters
[in]prefReference point.
[out]wiIncident direction.

Normalization \( A \).

\[ \begin{aligned} A &= \alpha s_x s_y \frac{1}{s_z^2} \\ &= \alpha \frac{s_x}{s_y} \cdot 4\tan^2\left[\frac{f}{2}\right] \end{aligned} \]

where \( \alpha = ({q_{\max}}_x - {q_{\min}}_x) ({q_{\max}}_y - {q_{\min}}_y) \)
