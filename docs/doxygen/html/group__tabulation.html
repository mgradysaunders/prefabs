Table linear interpolation in 2 dimensions.

  • \( \mu \gets (s - s_{[i - 1]}) / (s_{[i]} - s_{[i - 1]}) \)
  • \( \nu \gets (t - t_{[j - 1]}) / (t_{[j]} - t_{[j - 1]}) \)
  • \( f_0 \gets (1 - \nu) f_{[i - 1, j - 1]} + \nu f_{[i - 1, j]} \)
  • \( f_1 \gets (1 - \nu) f_{[i, j - 1]} + \nu f_{[i, j]} \)
  • \( (1 - \mu) f_0 + \mu f_1 \)
Precondition
  • \( m \ge 2 \)
  • \( n \ge 2 \)
  • \( s_{[i - 1]} \le s_{[i]} \) for all \( i \in [1, m) \)
  • \( t_{[j - 1]} \le t_{[j]} \) for all \( j \in [1, n) \)

  • \( g_{[0]} \gets 0 \)
  • \( g_{[j]} \gets g_{[j - 1]} + (t_{[j]} - t_{[j - 1]}) (f_{[j]} + f_{[j - 1]}) / 2 \)
